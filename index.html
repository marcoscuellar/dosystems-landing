The neuroscience behind the scaffold.

01 / THE 30% ORGANIZATION TAX
Fact

Research in Cognitive Load Theory shows neurodivergent brains spend up to 30% more metabolic energy just to organize a task before starting it.

OS Fix

Voice-to-text offloading bypasses this 'Transcription Tax,' saving your limited executive battery for the actual work.

02 / DECISION FATIGUE SUPPRESSION
Fact

Having more than 5 choices triggers Analysis Paralysis. Every flat list requires you to spend energy just deciding what is important.

OS Fix

The 'Build My Day' engine uses Automated Prioritization to collapse raw thoughts into a single, linear path. No more choosing — just following the scaffold.

03 / TRANSCRIPTION FRICTION
Fact

The gap between a high-speed thought and a typed word is a high-friction zone that triggers Task Paralysis and 'The 1 PM Wall.'

OS Fix

Stateless Offloading. Move thoughts at the speed of speech. No filing, no tagging, no friction. Just a clean exit for the mental noise.
